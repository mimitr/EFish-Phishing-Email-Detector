What is Phishing?

Phishing - is a scam that uses email to trick recipients into clicking on a link, opening attachment or otherwise taking action that produces harmful results.

So, is this a phishing email?

Top signs:

1. If the link attached in the email is marked as threatening, then it is definitely a phishing email. However, they might be new phishing links that our database have not been updated on yet, so do not entirely rely this.
2. If there are spelling and grammar errors
3. If the content of the email is of unusual or non-standard request
4. Check the sender's email reputation on EmailRep
